1. Hatha Yoga

Hatha Yoga is a gentle introduction to the basic yoga postures. Perfect for beginners and promotes relaxation.

2. Vinyasa Yoga

Vinyasa is a dynamic practice linking breath and movement. It builds strength and endurance.

3. Ashtanga Yoga

Ashtanga Yoga follows a specific sequence of poses practiced in a vigorous style. Best for experienced yogis.

4. Yin Yoga

Yin Yoga focuses on passive stretches held for longer periods. It improves flexibility and joint health.

5. Power Yoga

Power Yoga is a fitness-based yoga style emphasizing strength and flexibility through fast-paced flows.

6. Restorative Yoga

Restorative Yoga uses props to support the body in restful poses. Ideal for stress relief and recovery.

7. Kundalini Yoga

Kundalini Yoga combines breath, movement, and meditation to awaken spiritual energy within.

8. Iyengar Yoga

Iyengar Yoga emphasizes precision and alignment, often using props for support and posture correction.

9. Bikram Yoga

Bikram is a hot yoga style that includes a fixed sequence of 26 postures in a heated room.

10. Aerial Yoga

Aerial Yoga involves using a hammock to support poses, combining yoga with acrobatics and fun.

11. Prenatal Yoga

Designed for pregnant individuals, this style helps with flexibility, strength, and stress reduction.

12. Chair Yoga

Chair Yoga is a gentle form of yoga done while sitting or using a chair for support. Great for seniors.
